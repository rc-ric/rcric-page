Activities

Weekly Workshops

Weekly workshops provide an opportunity to learn robotics and modern technology. Members interact, design, build, and test ideas while sharing knowledge and solving problems together. The sessions support both beginners and experienced learners, helping them develop skills step by step in an engaging environment.

Robotics

Blend into the world of Robotics with the help of basic engineering, programming, and artificial intelligence to create machines that mimic human actions and solve real-world problems. From basic LFR to Rovers, Drones, etc., develop functional and modern robots.

Programming

Discover the exciting world of coding and see how computers and robots follow instructions. Learn the basics of programming using languages like Python, C++, and Arduino, and create fun projects. Turn lines of code into awesome robots, apps, and gadgets, and bring your ideas to life!

Web design & Development

Master the art of building modern interactive websites. By combining visual design with technical programming, learn the skills needed to build, host, and manage your own web projects from scratch.

Graphics and 3D Design

Dive deeply into the world of Robotics and ICT by empowering yourself to transform ideas into real prototypes using tools like Blender, Fusion 360, Canva, Adobe Illustrator, and more to design projects in 3d and with appealing graphics.

Competitions

Ignite your passion and skills in the areas of Robotics, AI, Design, Esports and many more in our thrilling competitions. Prove your brilliance and talent by participating in our competitive events and win prizes now. The biggest event we organized is EON Spectral Shift'17, the first national event on robotics in Bangladesh.

Active Project

SOLARIS V1

The Solaris V1 is a high-intensity Mars Rover Prototype, engineered to conquer extreme terrains through advanced robotic command. Inspired by planetary exploration, it utilizes a sophisticated Rocker-Bogie suspension system for stability. Equipped with a 7-DOF robotic arm and a real-time HD camera link, Solaris V1 executes manipulation tasks with tactical precision. At its core, an Arduino Mega 2560 drives the system.

Who We Are

Founded in 2016 and reformed in 2022, under the guidance of our Officer in Charge Md. Selim Ahmed, Rajuk College Robotics & ICT Club (RCRIC) serves as the place where students of Rajuk Uttara Model College can bring their technical ideas to life. Our club is a friendly space where you can learn and interact just by being curious about tech. Our goal is to equip students with real-world robotics and ICT skills that prepare them for the global tech industry. At RCRIC, we focus on learning by experimentation and observation, where our members get to work directly with hardware, write actual computer programs, and tackle real engineering problems. You don't need to be an expert to join, you just need to be curious.
